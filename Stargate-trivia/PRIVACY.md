# Privacy Policy 

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026

## Information We Collect
The Stargate Trivia Game ("the App") is a Devvit application built for Reddit. By interacting with the App, we collect the following information provided by the Reddit API:
- **Reddit Username:** Used exclusively to display your name on the Stargate leaderboard.
- **Game Scores:** The score you achieve during the trivia game is stored securely using Devvit's Redis infrastructure to maintain the subreddit's leaderboard.
- **Post Context:** We collect the Post ID where the interaction occurred to ensure scores are tied to the correct instance of the game.

## How We Use Your Information
- To operate the game and display the global high score leaderboard.
- We do **NOT** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. 
- We do **NOT** use your information for targeted advertising or tracking outside of Reddit.

## Data Retention
Scores and associated Reddit usernames are stored indefinitely in the App's Redis database to maintain historical leaderboards for the subreddit. If you wish to have your score or username removed from the Stargate Trivia leaderboard, please contact the developer or subreddit moderators.

## Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected in this document.

By using the Stargate Trivia Game, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
